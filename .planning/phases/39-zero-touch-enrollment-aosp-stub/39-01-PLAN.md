### Profile Assignment at Scale

[BODY per D-01 + RESEARCH.md Pitfall 5 — 90-120 words cross-linking `#link-zt-to-intune` Method A/B and surfacing the scale-operations implication WITHOUT restating Phase 35 content:]

Fleet profile-assignment MUST respect the Method A vs Method B choice documented in [Link Zero-Touch to Intune](#link-zt-to-intune):

- **COBO (Fully Managed) fleets**: Method A iframe is acceptable; the Fully Managed default is what you want.
