## Apple canonical 5-step procedure (from UAT.md root_cause — VERBATIM source of truth)

1. **Enable AssistiveTouch**: Settings > Accessibility > Touch > AssistiveTouch > toggle ON
2. **Add Analytics to Custom Top Level Menu**: Settings > Accessibility > Touch > AssistiveTouch > Customize Top Level Menu > tap Custom > select Analytics
3. **Trigger sysdiagnose**: tap on-screen AssistiveTouch button > Analytics (triggers sysdiagnose generation)
4. **Locate output files**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements > Analytics Data — scroll to `sysdiagnose_` prefixed files
5. **Export**: tap each sysdiagnose file > use share button (top-right) to send from device (AirDrop, email, iCloud, Files.app, etc.)

Authoritative Apple Support URL: https://support.apple.com/guide/platform-support/use-diagnostics-to-research-device-issues-supd3f43814e/web
